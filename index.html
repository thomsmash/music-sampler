Driftless Pony Club is an indie rock band featuring vocals from YouTuber Craig Benzine (WheezyWaiter) based in Chicago, Illinois. Their lyrics feature a distinct surreal style aided by grungy guitar playing and rocking drums. Songs like "House of 1982 Built Like a Ship" and "Legends of Archery" have been used in a number of DFTBA projects, so don't be surprised if their music sounds familiar.
